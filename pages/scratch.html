Places I've Visited



Vancouver, Canada

This little spot is called Deep Cove Park, and it is GOURGEOUS!



Monterosso al Mare, Italy

Monterosso is a little slice of heaven on Earth. Wonderful people, wonderful food, and in such a beautiful location, right up against the Lagurian Sea. Be sure to stick around for the Anchovy Festival held here once a year!



Český Krumlov, Czech Republic

A quaint little town known for...pencils? Believe it or not, the Koh-i-Noor Hardtmuth company, a world-renowned producer of pencils and other art supplies, is found here!



Munich, Germany

The capital of Bavaria, and home to the world-famous Glockenspiel! Munich is a city with rich history (and even richer food). Look below for a video showing the Glockenspiel in action!
